India EV Growth & Infrastructure Analysis

🎯 Project Objective:
To develop a comprehensive analytics dashboard that evaluates EV adoption trends across India and identifies infrastructure gaps, enabling better understanding of regional demand and charging network readiness.

📊 Dashboards Included

1. 📍 Executive Overview

Analyzes overall EV growth and provides a high-level view of adoption trends across India.

Highlights:

KPI Cards: Total EV, Charging Stations, EV per Charging Station

Trends: EV Adoption & YoY Growth (2021–2025)

Distribution: EV adoption across states

Interactive Filters: State, Year


Key Insights:

• EV adoption reached 26M vehicles by 2025, showing consistent growth

• States like Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra lead in total EV adoption

• Growth trend indicates increasing acceleration in EV demand


2. ⚡ Infrastructure Gap Analysis

Evaluates whether charging infrastructure is keeping pace with EV demand.

Highlights:

Scatter Plot: EV demand vs charging stations

Classification: High Stress, Moderate, Good

KPI Cards: Count of states by infrastructure category

Quadrant View: Identifies critical gap regions

Drillthrough enabled for deeper analysis

Key Insights:

• Several states fall under High Stress, indicating insufficient infrastructure

• Some states show underutilized infrastructure despite lower demand

• Infrastructure expansion is not evenly aligned with EV adoption


3. 🔍 State Deep Dive (Drillthrough)

Provides detailed analysis for a selected state to understand local trends.

Highlights:

KPI Cards: Total EV, Charging Stations, EV per Station, Infra Status

Trends: EV growth and infrastructure pressure over time

State Ranking based on infrastructure stress

Insight Card: Actionable recommendation

Key Insights:

• High EV per station indicates urgent need for charging expansion

• State-level trends highlight differences in adoption vs infrastructure growth


4. 🚘 EV Category Analysis (Drillthrough)

Breaks down EV adoption by vehicle type to identify demand drivers.

Highlights:

KPI Cards: Total EV (2W, 3W, 4W)

Trends: Category-wise growth over time

Distribution: Percentage share by vehicle segment

State-level drillthrough support

Key Insights:

• Two-wheelers dominate EV adoption (>80%)

• Three-wheelers contribute moderately to growth

• Four-wheelers show gradual adoption, indicating early-stage expansion

🔍 Key Business Insights

• EV adoption is growing rapidly across India

• Infrastructure development is uneven and lagging in high-demand states

• Two-wheelers are the primary driver of EV growth

• Strategic infrastructure planning is required at a regional level


🛠 Tools Used
Power BI Desktop
Power Query (ETL)
DAX (calculated measures & logic)
Excel (data source)
